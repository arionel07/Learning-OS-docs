## What is Learnin OS?

Learnin OS is a modular learning platform designed to help you build real skills through structured documentation, hands-on projects, and interactive modules.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced developer looking to level up — Learnin OS has a path for you.

## What you'll find here

- **Getting Started** — Install, configure, and run your first project in minutes
- **Concepts** — Understand the core architecture and philosophy behind Learnin OS
- **Modules** — Explore individual learning modules and how to build your own
